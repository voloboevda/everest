# Customer brief — Everest Trade landing

Source: `everest_trade_landing_structure.docx`

## Concept

Single-page corporate landing for an international trading company.

**Hero line:**  
Международная торговая компания на рынках промышленных, энергетических и сельскохозяйственных ресурсов.

**Languages:** RU · ENG · CHI (中文). Switcher top-right.

## Blocks

| # | Section | Content |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | Hero | Port/ship/terminal bg. CTAs: Отправить запрос, Связаться с нами |
| 2 | About | Global commodity markets; Asia, Europe, CIS partners |
| 3 | Sectors | Metals, energy, agro, minerals/chemicals, intl trade & agency |
| 4 | Expertise | Deal structuring, import/export, logistics, trade finance, contracts |
| 5 | Geography | World map; Russia, China, India, SEA, Middle East — equal weight |
| 6 | Logistics | FOB, CIF, CPT; container + bulk cargo |
| 7 | Cooperation | Buyers list / Producers list (per docx) |
| 8 | RFQ form | Name, company, country, email, message |
